Optimum Sit Amet

Gear up as you play the devil’s advocate and go neck and neck against the contenders from other teams, debating against and contradicting not just your opposition but even yourself?! Feel the rush as you speak in favour of as well as against the topic provided to you on-the-spot while being critiqued on your content, confidence and spontaneity. There’s no room for error as you attempt to sweep through the competition and clench victory. So, will you rise to the occasion or fall prey to the heat of the moment and fumble? Only one way to find out.

Number of rounds: 2 .

Event for individual participants: maximum 3 from each school .

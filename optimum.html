Optimum Sit Amet

Let it be the most unexpected situation or a crisis a good manager knows to deal with the pressure. Through this exciting event of best manager, participants will be challenged and tested through every practical aspect of the problems that a manager must deal with in a regular course of business in two extremely exciting rounds. Optimum Sit Amet

ABOUT THE EVENT:

The aim of this event is to identify in students the traits of a good manager. It also aims to find out how he/she demonstrates the skill, knowledge, and talents of a good manager. It further tests how he/she uses the strategies in the time of crises and personal capabilities of excellence.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

1. Per school only 2 participants are allowed- INDIVIUAL EVENT

2. There will be 2 rounds held.

3. A resume (for the job interview) must be made by each participant before the event starts.

ROUND 1: STRESS INTERVIEW

(Day: 1- this round will be over by 12:00 pm)

Without an excellent interview it is not possible for a good manager to make an impact on the business. It is not the interviewer or the questionaries that determine the judgement but it is the self-confidence of the applicant.

So, we are going to provide them a first-hand experience to a managerial job interview.

The participants are required to forward their resume to the designated email sent.

Then each participant will be called into an individual meeting headed by one of the event organizers (Event head 1,Event head 2,Event head 3 ).

Just for example:

From 9:30 – 10:00 =Event head 1- Participant 1

Event head 2- Participant 2

Event head 3- Participant 3

From 10:00 – 10:30 =Event head 1- Participant 4  

Event head 2- Participant 5

Event head 3- Participant 6

........and so, on.........

Questions are going to be asked which will provide a real-life job interview experience to participants.

(Counter questions are also included)

Participants will be assessed on the following criteria in this round:

- Accuracy in answers

- Resume

- Time management

- Confidence

- Reasoning skills

Participants with highest scores will be advanced to the next and final round.

ROUND 2: CRISIS MANAGEMENT

(Day 1- after 1:00 pm till the end of the day)

(Day 2- morning 9:00 am to 11:00 am)

The primary role of a manager is to handle situations during a crisis. His/her skills and patience are tested and refined over a period of time.

On day 1, after the first round 6 finalists will be chosen. They all will be called up in a meeting any time after 1:00pm. then they will be given 5 case studies each for which they have to write down the answer and mail it to us.

The answers of the finalists will be forwarded to the judges who will analyse their answer.

On day 2, a meeting will be held in which the judges will be asking the finalists counter questions based on the answers they sent previously.

And after all this, judges will choose the winner of the best manager event based on their scores/marks.

Participants will be assessed on the following criteria in this round:

- Interpersonal/communication skills [while answering the counter questions]

- Logical reasoning

- Professional impression

- Motivation/initiative

- Candidates understanding of the position

- Creative approach

Turpis Tristique

Do you have what it takes to convince the sharks to invest in your idea to make it the next big thing? Our event, Turpis Tristique brings to you a platform to test your entrepreneurial skills. From coming up with an innovative idea that doesn’t exist to making a complete business plan to presenting it to the investors, this event will challenge all you know about building your own venture. Requirements: Fair knowledge in making a business plan.

No. of participants per team: 2

No. of teams per school: 3

No. of rounds:2

Turpis Tristique

As we all know Shark Tank is a popular show where the sharks hear pitches from business owners who want venture capital funding from the sharks. The sharks typically require a stake in the business, which is a percentage of ownership and a share of the profits. In return for giving up partial ownership in their company, the entrepreneur gets funding, but often, more importantly, they get access to the sharks, their network of contacts, suppliers, and experience.

This event is somewhat similar to this widely popular show. Competitors will be asked to make a pitch for their self- made product which does not already exist in the market. This even mainly consists of two rounds which will be described in detail in the following paragraphs.

The main aim in planning this event is to help cultivate young talent in the wide field of entrepreneurship and install a sense of motivation and confidence in their ideas. The present generation is highly advanced in many aspects such as availability of required guidance, resources and technology. All this can be used in a very useful manner to help the young generation to focus on the current needs and demands of the people as they can come up with new ideas. We hope to see very attractive and out of this world ideas.

The “sharks” will be judging the participants based on the following criteria:

•Level of innovation

• Level of innovation

• Similarity to existing product in the market

• Attractive advertising techniques

• Methods by which participants solve the problems given to them

• Budget and optimum utilisation of resources

• Managerial knowledge

• Pressure test

• Teamwork

• Way of pitch for investment

• Stake the “Sharks” would get in their business

These are the following fields:

• Healthcare – Showcase your venture which is related or helpful towards healthcare, both physical and mental, which could be a possible enterprise. In this age of selfcare on focusing on the self, ideas could relate to that too.

• Fashion Technology – an innovative touch to fashion like never seen before! The range is wide! Show us something that would make a great addition to the world of fashion. Participants could Come up with sustainable fashion which is kind towards the earth. Small businesses with affordable and trendy items are supported too!

• Digital innovation – A great chance for all the future IT aspirants and engineers to display their talents and make their innovations known to world! Come up with new gadgets like never seen before which would help people across the world and which is affordable by all.

• Food science – Who doesn’t love food!? Bring out the best cuisines in the world and a chance to tap into your inner culinary skills! Tweaking existing dishes to make them better or adding a western twist to Indian dishes, you decide!

• Social affairs – Display a business venture for a greater cause! Show ways by which you could bring about betterment to society. There is a lot of poverty and social issues around the globe. Come up with innovations which could possibly lead to the eradication of these!

• Service sector – A service rendering venture like never seen before! Be it an app or a software or a service, its all up to you! Think of the prevailing demands of people in this world full of hustle and bustle, we’re sure you can come up with something that would make everyone’s life easier!

TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

• The number of participants in one group can range from 1 to 3

• The idea or innovation must comply to at least one field mentioned

• Combination of fields is allowed

• Copying of existing ideas is prohibited as it is against the purpose pf the competition and can lead to disqualification

• Ventures must be presented in a professional way and the pitch must be made in a smart way to attract the attention of the “sharks”

Procedure

There will be two rounds in this event. Round 1 will be held on day 1 and round 2 on day 2 of the fest.

Round 1 – This round will mainly cover the product, place and production aspects of the business. Here, the participants will have to pitch in their ideas to the judges covering the above parts.

Round 2 – In this round the participants will have to explain the price aspect of the business. This will include the entire business plan prepared by the participants. After hearing the participants, the judges have the liberty to ask them questions regarding their business. The judges will then propose a situation to the participants for which the participants will have to come up with a solution for.

During round 1, the organizing team will check the authenticity of the product and verify if the product does not exist already.

After round 1, the participants will be shortlisted based on the results from the judges.

In case of case of duplication of any product that already exists before, the participants will be eliminated from the event.

Pre-requisites – Develop a complete business plan prior to the event.

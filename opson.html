OPSONATUS

Do you remember the Happydent palace ad from 2007? The one where men had to smile to light up a dark room?  That advertisement was clever and hilarious, one that we remember even 10 years later. Are you capable of coming up with something so hysterical and brilliant? Creativity, marketing and problem-solving skills should be your strong point. Are you talented enough to entice customers through social media? Are you adept at creating a masterpiece? Register to know.  

No of participants per group: 3 

No of teams per school: 2

No of Rounds: 2 (first round being an elimination round)

×

OPSONATUS

Rules:

1. The video should not exceed 2 minutes.

2. It should not contain inappropriate gestures or words. In case music is used, it should not contain unparliamentary language.

3. Controversial opinions, fear mongering, mudslinging should be avoided.

4. All opinions expressed in the video are the opinions of individual members only and has nothing to do with the event.

5. Top 5 teams will move on to round 2.

6. It should be self-produced.

7. Top 5 teams will be qualified for round 2.

8. The deadline for entries is 11:59pm on 6th January.  

Round 1: The Arbiter’s Approval

Duration: Each group will have 10 minutes for presentation – 2 minutes for the video, 2 minutes for explanation and 1 minute for judge’s queries. 5 minutes in case of technical delays. Maximum duration is 3 hours.

Criteria:

- Creativity – originality and eccentricity

- Content – information, awareness and humour.

- Ability to attract audience’s attention.

Round 2: Decipher the Dissension

Duration: Each group will have 10 minutes for presentation – 2 minutes for analysing the problem, 3 minutes for coming up with a solution. 5 minutes in case of technical delays. Maximum duration is 1 hour.

Criteria:

- Practicality

- Creativity

- Relevance

Round1:

Participants will be given a genre of products to choose from. Once they decide, they will have to create a reel/igtv(1-2mins) advertising their product, which should cater to social media marketing. Participants can choose to act in it, make an animation, or do anything else that will portray their creativity. They will be required to send their submissions 2-3 days before the fest. This video will be posted on our official Instagram page. They will also be required to present this video to the judges during the fest, further explaining and marketing their product, thus convincing the judges. The submission that gets the highest number of interactions and positive feedback will be qualified for the next round. The participants will be judged based on creative thinking, intelligence and how entertaining their video was.   

Round2:  

The participants qualified for the next round will be given a brand or product that has faced some type of controversy or conflict in the past. The participants will be given 3-5 minutes to think of an efficient solution. This activity will be based on their problem-solving skills. Once they come up with an answer, they will be required to present it to the judges. The winner will be chosen based on their quick problem-solving skills and intellect.

Sample questions:

Round 1:

1.What are your products/ service’s key benefits?

2. Who is the intended target audience for your ad?

3. What is your single key message in one sentence?

4. What is the key objective of this campaign?

5. What problems are you solving?

6. What is your tone?

Round 2:

1.Do you think people are overreacting?

2. How do you plan to deal with the financial loss?

3. Do you think controversial content marketing works?

4. How do you plan to take responsibility?

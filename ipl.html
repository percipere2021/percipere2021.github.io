Mega IPL Auction

Use your analytical and managerial skills to form the best team with the top rated players from all over the globe. So if you love the game and follow IPL, this is your chance to convert your cricket and analytical knowledge into a winning bidding strategy.

Mega IPL Auction

Description of the event

The “Mock IPL Auction” is a simulation of real IPL auction. Participants will step in the shoes of the stakeholders of IPL teams and they will bid for players they wish to add to their respective teams from a diverse pool of players. 

Note : Number of participants in each team: 3 members.

Round 1

Description: A Quiz on cricket /IPL and finance management to test ability and knowledge of the teams. The quiz will consist of 15- 20 questions that have to be answered within a particular time frame.

Aspects of the quiz:

1. General Trivia of the IPL 

2. Questions regarding Twenty20 cricket in general 

3. Questions on the Business and Finance aspect of the various teams of the IPL 

Round 2

Description: After the eliminatory first round, 8 teams are selected for the second round. In this event the participating teams will be provided with a maximum limit (budget) of virtual funds which they can used to buy the players for their teams. All the teams will be presented with the player‘s profile, rating and a minimum bid. The teams will have to bid for their desired players and the team with the highest bid will get the player.

This event will test how the participants use their funds effectively and efficiently, while filling every required position in their team.

Rules:

Round 1:

1. The eliminatory quiz will consist of 20 multiple choice questions based on the topics listed above.

2. In case of a tie in marks of the participating teams, the team which answered the tiebreaker questions will be selected.

3. Resorting to unfair means of any kind will lead to a detract disqualification from the event.

4. There will be no negative marking for a wrong answer.

5. The top 8 teams from this round will qualify for the second and final round where the mock IPL auction will take place.

4. Teams can start bidding from a base price. In case of a tie, the first bid will be considered.
